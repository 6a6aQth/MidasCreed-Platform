# MidasCreed — Public Positioning (v1)

Use this as source material for the website, LinkedIn, and Twitter/X rebuild. Everything here is grounded in real, current capability — nothing aspirational, nothing describing a platform or client base that doesn't exist yet. Update it as real work adds new true things to say.

## One-liner

MidasCreed helps businesses automate the repetitive parts of their operations with AI agents — built, deployed, and maintained, not just recommended.

## Mission

To make practical AI automation accessible to businesses of any size — from a single-location shop to an international operation — by finding the one workflow that matters most and making it work, rather than selling a strategy deck.

## What we do

**AI Readiness Assessment.** We look at how a business actually runs today and identify where AI can help — and where it can't. Delivered through the MidasCreed Enterprise AI Adoption Framework, which maps any organization to one of four maturity levels (Awareness, Enablement, Integration, Infrastructure) and scopes the right next step from there.

**AI Agent Implementation.** We build working AI agents — systems that read incoming requests, draft responses, update records, and handle repetitive coordination work — using best-in-class infrastructure (Claude and other leading models, Composio for tool connectivity, Hermes Agent for persistent execution). We're implementation-first: a working system within days, not a proposal.

**Managed AI Operations.** Once an agent is live, we maintain it — fixing issues, extending capability, adapting it as the business changes — as an ongoing service rather than a one-time project.

## How we work

Every engagement starts the same way: a focused audit of one real workflow, not a broad strategy exercise. We find the automation with the most value and the least effort, build it, prove it works, and only then talk about what's next. Pricing and engagement scale to the size of the business we're working with — a small operation and a large one don't pay the same, and shouldn't.

## Who we serve

Growing businesses — local and international — looking for practical automation rather than an AI strategy document. We work with businesses at any stage of AI adoption, including those who haven't used AI tools in any structured way yet.

## What makes this different

- **We build, not just advise.** The deliverable is working software running in your operations, not a report.
- **We start small on purpose.** One proven workflow before a bigger conversation, not a large upfront commitment.
- **We're not locked into one vendor's stack.** We assemble the right tools for the job rather than selling a single proprietary platform.
- **Pricing that matches the business, not a fixed enterprise rate card.**

## Voice for all public content

- Plain, outcome-first language. Say what problem goes away, not what technology is involved.
- No unearned comparisons to larger firms, no claims about clients or sectors we haven't actually served.
- Founder-led tone where it fits (LinkedIn posts, Twitter) — real people building real things, not a faceless brand voice.
- Every claim should be true today, not true of where the company is heading. If it's not true yet, it doesn't go here.
